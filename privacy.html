Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Proalab Limited ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use the Delivery Driver Wage Calculator website (the "Service"). This Service is operated in association with MyWay Route Planner.

Information We Collect

Calculator Data: All calculations are performed locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the financial information you enter into our calculator. Your earnings, hours, miles, and expense data never leave your device.

Analytics Data: We may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site. This may include:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website

Cookies: We employ the use of cookies to enhance your experience. By accessing this website, you agree to use cookies in accordance with this Privacy Policy. Most interactive websites use cookies to retrieve user details for each visit. Cookies are used by our website to enable functionality of certain areas and to make it easier for people visiting our website.

Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure. Since we do not collect or store your calculator data, there is no risk of your financial information being compromised through our Service. All calculations happen entirely within your browser. Use the Service at your own risk.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 16 years of age, or the minimum legal age in your jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. Since our calculator does not store any personal data, most of these rights are automatically satisfied. For any analytics-related data inquiries, please contact us.
